Description: Monthly temperature at NH 75 degree from 1856 to 2001. 

The data are for the monthly Northern Hemisphere global temperature (in
C°) at 75 degree N, from January 1856 to December 2001, 
anomalies w.r.t. the monthly averages during 1961 to 1990. The data were downloaded
from the Climate Research Unit (CRU), University of East Anglia.
